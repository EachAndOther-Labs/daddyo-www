Labour Checklist

The list

  • Don't leave this checklist till the last minute. Make sure that you've prepared a couple of weeks in advance of the due date.

  • Know the route to the hospital from common places you might be.

  • Know what parking is available at the hospital or nearby.

  • All the important numbers you need are in your phone contacts AND on a paper backup.

  • Car seat is properly installed in the car. (There's a good chance you'll be going home alone for a few days after the baby is born so you might have a chance to install it then too.)

  • Hospital dad-bag is packed (including change for parking meters).

  • Hospital lady-bag is packed.

  • Contractions kit: Stopwatch, contraction chart and pen.

  • Freezer and fridge well stocked with easy to cook, nutritious food.
