Finding out you're going to be a dad can be a bit overwhelming. But don't panic, help is at hand. DADDYO will help you from bump to birth and 6 months on. Now let's get to work.

Distills all the top info into one app

You don't have to read a huge stack of books. We've crammed everything you need to know into one handy app.

PRO-TIPS FROM GUYS WHO'VE BEEN THERE

Get the real stuff you need to know, from dads who've learnt the hard way so you don't have to.

SIMPLE PRACTICAL HOW-TOS

We've broken things down into simple steps so you can be prepared for any situation that might be thrown at you.

FOLLOW THE TIMELINE

Use the timeline to get the right info you need when you need it. DADDYO app is designed to help you from the first trimester through to caring for your baby in the first 6 months.

About the app

The app was originated by iQ Content’s Tom Cunningham, whose personal experience was the catalyst:

“As a first time dad, I found the books to be a bit of a chore. I wanted something that showed me how to be a good dad and was always there just when I needed it. Thats why we created DADDYO.
